ToW3D proposes a consistency-aware framework for interactive point-based 3D mesh editing on GANs. The method enables users to interactively manipulate 3D meshes through intuitive point-based controls while maintaining geometric and texture consistency throughout the editing process. By leveraging the latent space of pre-trained GANs, ToW3D achieves high-quality and semantically meaningful mesh deformations with consistent multi-view appearance.
